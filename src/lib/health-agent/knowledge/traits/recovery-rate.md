# Recovery Rate

How quickly your body repairs and regenerates after exercise, stress, or illness. Driven by mitochondrial health and anabolic hormone levels.

## Derivation Rules

- **Slow**: MitoScore below 50 OR Testosterone below optimal OR IGF-1 below optimal
- **Moderate**: MitoScore 50-75 OR some hormone markers sub-optimal
- **Fast**: MitoScore above 75 AND key hormones within optimal range

## What This Means for the User

When recovery rate is **slow**, the user may experience:
- Extended muscle soreness after workouts (48+ hours)
- Fatigue that doesn't resolve with rest
- Slower wound healing
- Decreased exercise tolerance over time

When recovery rate is **fast**, the body efficiently repairs tissue, replenishes energy stores, and adapts to training stimulus.
