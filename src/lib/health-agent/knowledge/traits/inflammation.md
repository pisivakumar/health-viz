# Inflammation Tendency

Chronic low-grade inflammation is linked to virtually every major disease. Your markers suggest how much systemic inflammation your body carries.

## Derivation Rules

- **High**: WBC OR AST OR ALT above optimal range (2+ markers out)
- **Moderate**: One marker near the upper boundary
- **Low**: All markers within optimal range

## What This Means for the User

When inflammation tendency is **high**, the user may experience:
- Slower recovery from exercise and illness
- Joint stiffness or generalized aches
- Liver stress (elevated AST/ALT)
- Increased oxidative damage to cells
- Higher risk of chronic conditions over time

When inflammation tendency is **low**, the body's immune response is well-regulated.
